Is it safe? Are there any viruses?
Absolutely. Avenor is clean software. We do not embed ads, hidden miners, or toolbars. You can check the installer with any antivirus (e.g., VirusTotal) before running it.
What formats are supported?
You can download videos in MP4, MKV, WebM and audio in MP3, M4A, WAV. There is also a built-in converter that lets you recode downloaded files into any convenient format for your phone or player.
Can I download an entire playlist or channel?

The app features a cookies import function (Netscape format). If you have access to the video in your browser, you can download it via Avenor using your cookies. This works for private videos, age-restricted content, and paid subscriptions (e.g., YouTube Premium).
